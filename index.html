A welcoming space for your event

With space for 30 people and optional use of tables or chairs you can adapt our space to meet your event's needs. We're open for one-off or regular bookings every day of the week.

Main hall area with chairs and foldable tables set up for a meeting, with kitchen behind

An adaptable space

Warm and cosy, functional and clean
Seating options

Comfortable seating for meetings of 20 or more, and larger arm chairs for parties, discussion groups and informal meetings.

Light, bright and a garden

With doors opening to our private garden you have flexibility if the weather is good to let in plenty of fresh air or extend your event to the outdoors.

Fully equipped kitchen

An oven and microwave allow warming of food, or use the tea urn, kettles and fridge for tea and coffee. We also currently offer laundry facilities.

WiFi, audio and video kit

Fast WiFi is open to all hirers and can be used with our audio amplifier and speakers for background music or presentations. We also have a projector and microphones available.
